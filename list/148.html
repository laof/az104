Question : 108 Topic: 2

HOTSPOT

-
You have a Microsoft Entra tenant that contains the users shown in the following table.
The tenant contains the groups shown in the following table.
Which users and groups can be deleted? To answer, select the appropriate options in the answer area.
NOTE: Each correct selection is worth one point.

Suggested Answer:

Correct Answer:

Users: User1, User2, User3, and User4
Groups: Group1 only
-----------------

Explanation of Correct Options:

User1, User2, User3, and User4:
All user accounts can be deleted regardless of whether they have a license assigned. Licensing does not prevent deletion. However, deleting a licensed user will revoke their access to services.
Group1 only:
Group1 has no license and is not a parent or child of any other group. It is safe to delete without affecting other groups or users.
-----------------

Explanation of Other Options:

User1 only:
While User1 can be deleted, this option excludes other users who are also eligible for deletion.
User3 and User4 only:
This excludes User1 and User2, both of whom can also be deleted.
User2 and User4 only:
Again, this excludes User1 and User3, who are also eligible for deletion.
Group4 only:
Group4 has a Microsoft Entra ID P2 license assigned. Licensed groups cannot be deleted.
Group3 and Group4 only:
Group3 is a parent of Group2, and Group4 has a license. Neither can be deleted.
Group1, Group2, Group3, and Group4:
Group2 and Group4 have licenses, and Group3 is part of a nested group structure. Only Group1 is safe to delete.
