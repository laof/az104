Correct Answers:

The minimum number of copies of the storage account will be: 3
To reduce cost if infrequently accessed data in this account, you must modify the: Access tier
Explanation: Locally-redundant storage (LRS) keeps 3 copies of your data within a single data center to ensure durability. The Access tier setting determines the cost model based on how frequently data is accessed; switching from Hot to Cool or Archive reduces costs for infrequent access.
-----------------
Other replication options like GRS or ZRS provide more redundancy but are not used here; LRS is the most basic with 3 local copies.
Other settings such as Performance, Account kind, or Networking do not directly affect cost for infrequently accessed data; only the Access tier setting does.
