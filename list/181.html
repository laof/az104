Question : 27 Topic: 3

You have an app named App1 that runs on two Azure virtual machines named VM1 and VM2.

You plan to implement an Azure Availability Set for App1. The solution must ensure that App1 is available during planned maintenance of the hardware hosting
VM1 and VM2.
What should you include in the Availability Set?

  • A..one update domain
  • B..two fault domains
  • C..one fault domain
  • CorrectD..two update domains
Suggested Answer:

Question 3-27 Answer:

Correct Answer: D. two update domains
-----------------
Explanation of correct option:
Update domains ensure that during planned maintenance, not all virtual machines are rebooted at the same time. By placing VM1 and VM2 in different update domains, you ensure that at least one VM remains available during maintenance, which meets the requirement.
Explanation of other options:
A. one update domain: If both VMs are in the same update domain, they could be rebooted together during maintenance, which would cause downtime.
B. two fault domains: Fault domains protect against unplanned hardware failures, not planned maintenance. This does not meet the requirement.
C. one fault domain: This offers no redundancy for unplanned failures and does not address planned maintenance scenarios.
