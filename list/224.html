You have an Azure Storage account named storage1.
You need to enable a user named User1 to list and regenerate storage account keys for storage1.
Solution: You assign the Storage Account Encryption Scope Contributor Role to User1.
Does this meet the goal?

  • A..Yes
  • CorrectB..No
Suggested Answer:
B..No

Explanation of right option:

Storage Account Encryption Scope Contributor does not grant permissions to list or regenerate storage account keys. This role only allows managing encryption scopes within storage accounts, not managing account keys.
-----------------
A..Yes: No because this role does not provide permissions to manage or view storage account keys. To list and regenerate keys, User1 needs the Storage Account Key Operator Service Role or Owner/Contributor role on the storage account.
