You have an Azure Storage account named storage1.
You need to enable a user named User1 to list and regenerate storage account keys for storage1.
Solution: You assign the Reader and Data Access role to User1.
Does this meet the goal?

  • A..Yes
  • CorrectB..No
Suggested Answer:

Correct answer: B..No

B..No — Correct. The Reader and Data Access role allows User1 to view storage account data and read properties, but it does not grant permissions to list or regenerate storage account keys. To manage keys, the user needs the Owner, Contributor, or Storage Account Key Operator Service Role at the storage account scope.

A..Yes — Incorrect. The Reader and Data Access role does not provide permissions to list or regenerate keys. Additional privileges are required for these actions.
