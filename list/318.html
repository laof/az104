Question : 66 Topic: 4

You have web apps in the West US, Central US and East US Azure regions.

You have the App Service plans shown in the following table.
You plan to create an additional App Service plan named ASP5 that will use the Linux operating system.
You need to identify in which of the currently used locations you can deploy ASP5.
What should you recommend?

  • CorrectA..West US, Central US, or East US
  • B..Central US only
  • C..East US only
  • D..West US only
Suggested Answer:
A..West US, Central US, or East US
A..West US, Central US, or East US - You can create a new Linux App Service plan in any region where you don't already have a Linux plan, and all three regions are already in use. West US is valid since there is only a Windows plan there, so a Linux plan can also be created in that region. Central US and East US already have Linux plans, and you can create additional Linux plans in those regions as well.
-----------------
B..Central US only - Incorrect because you are not limited to only Central US for Linux plans.
C..East US only - Incorrect because you are not limited to only East US for Linux plans.
D..West US only - Incorrect because, while you can create a Linux plan in West US, you are not limited to only that region; you can also use Central US and East US.
