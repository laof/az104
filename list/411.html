Suggested Answer:
NSG1 limits VM1 traffic  No
NSG1 applies to VM2  Yes

Explanation: NSG1 does not limit VM1 traffic because, based on the scenario, NSG1 is applied to the subnet of VM2, not VM1. VM1 and VM2 connect to the same virtual network because both subnets (172.16.1.0/24 and 172.16.2.0/24) are part of the same VNet.
