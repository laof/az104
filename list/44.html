Correct Answer:

Explanation:
The review is scoped to Group1 and limited to guest users only. User3 is the owner of Group1 and thus the reviewer. Although UserB is a guest and a member of Group2 (which is nested in Group1), access reviews do not affect nested group memberships. Therefore, User3 cannot update UserBâ€™s access through this review.
Option: User3 can perform an access review of User1: Incorrect because User1 is a member, not a guest.
Option: User3 can perform an access review of UserA: Incorrect because UserA is also a member.
Option: User3 can perform an access review of UserB: Incorrect because UserB is a guest in a nested group, and access reviews do not remove nested group memberships.
