Suggested Answer:
Statement 1: The A record for VM5 will be registered automatically in the adatum.com zone. No
Explanation: The registration virtual network for adatum.com is VNet2. Only VMs in the registration network (VNet2) have their DNS records automatically registered in the private DNS zone. VM5 is in VNet1, so its A record will not be registered automatically.
Statement 2: VM5 can resolve VM9.adatum.com. No
Explanation: There is no evidence in the information or screenshots provided that VNet1 is linked to the adatum.com private DNS zone for resolution. Without this link, VM5 cannot resolve records in the zone, including VM9.adatum.com.
Statement 3: VM6 can resolve VM9.adatum.com. Yes
Explanation: VM6 is in VNet2, which is the registration virtual network for the zone. The A record for VM9 is present in the DNS zone, so VM6 can resolve VM9.adatum.com.
