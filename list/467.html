Explanation:
Contoso.com is a public DNS zone and does not auto-register VM A records; assigning the Owner role does not add DNS records.
Fabrikam.com is a private DNS zone with auto-registration enabled for vnet1, so both VMs in vnet1 will have their private IPs automatically registered as A records in fabrikam.com.
