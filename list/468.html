You have an on-premises datacenter and an Azure subscription.

You plan to connect the datacenter to Azure by using ExpressRoute.
You need to deploy an ExpressRoute gateway. The solution must meet the following requirements:
✑ Support up to 10 Gbps of traffic.
✑ Support availability zones.
✑ Support FastPath.
✑ Minimize costs.
Which SKU should you deploy?

  • A..ERGw1AZ
  • B..ERGw2
  • C..ErGw3
  • CorrectD..ErGw3AZ
Suggested Answer:
D. ErGw3AZ
Explanation: ErGw3AZ (ExpressRoute Gateway v3 with Availability Zones) supports up to 10 Gbps throughput, availability zones, FastPath, and is the most cost-effective SKU that meets all requirements; lower SKUs do not meet all features.
Other options:
A. ERGw1AZ does not support 10 Gbps or FastPath;
B. ERGw2 does not support 10 Gbps, availability zones, or FastPath;
C. ErGw3 supports 10 Gbps and FastPath but does not support availability zones.
