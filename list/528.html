Question : 5 Topic: 6

You have an Azure subscription that contains the identities shown in the following table.

User1, Principal1, and Group1 are assigned the Monitoring Reader role.
An action group named AG1 has the Email Azure Resource Manager Role notification type and is configured to email the Monitoring Reader role.
You create an alert rule named Alert1 that uses AG1.
You need to identity who will receive an email notification when Alert1 is triggered.
Who should you identify?

  • A..User1 and Principal1 only
  • B..User1, User2, Principal1, and Principal2
  • CorrectC..User1 only
  • D..User1 and User2 only
Suggested Answer:
C..User1 only

Explanation of Right Options

User1 is the only individual directly assigned the Monitoring Reader role who has a valid email address; according to the saying, email notifications will not be sent to Azure AD groups or service principals (including managed identities like Principal1), so only User1 will receive the email notification when Alert1 is triggered.
-----------------

Explanation of Other Options

A..User1 and Principal1 only — Incorrect, as Principal1 is a managed identity (service principal) and will not receive emails.
B..User1, User2, Principal1, and Principal2 — Incorrect, as User2 is not directly assigned the Monitoring Reader role, and managed identities and group members do not receive email notifications.
D..User1 and User2 only — Incorrect, as User2 only has the role via group membership and will not be notified.
