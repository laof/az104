You create a Recovery Services vault backup policy named Policy1 as shown in the following exhibit:
Use the drop-down menus to select the answer choice that completes each statement based on the information presented in the graphic.
NOTE: Each correct selection is worth one point.

Suggested Answer:>
The backup that occurs on Sunday, March 1, will be retained for 10 years.
The backup that occurs on Sunday, November 1, will be retained for 36 months.
Explanation of right options:
10 years: The policy retains yearly backup points for 10 years. Since March 1 is the scheduled yearly backup date, the backup on Sunday, March 1, will be retained for 10 years.
36 months: The policy retains monthly backup points for 36 months. November 1 is the first day of the month and is a monthly backup, so the backup on Sunday, November 1, will be retained for 36 months.
-----------------
30 days: This is the retention for daily backup points, but March 1 and November 1 match longer retention (yearly/monthly) according to the policy and thus are not retained for just 30 days.
10 weeks: This is the retention for weekly backup points, but since the backups in question coincide with monthly/yearly retention schedules, they are kept longer per the policy.
