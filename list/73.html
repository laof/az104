You need to ensure that an Azure Active Directory (Azure AD) user named Admin1 is assigned the required role to enable Traffic Analytics for an Azure subscription.
Solution: You assign the Network Contributor role at the subscription level to Admin1.
Does this meet the goal?

  • A..Yes
  • CorrectB..No
Suggested Answer:

Question 2-33

Correct Answer: B. No – The Network Contributor role allows managing network resources but does not grant access to Log Analytics, which is required to enable Traffic Analytics. To meet the goal, Admin1 needs a role like Log Analytics Contributor or Reader on the Log Analytics workspace.
-----------------
Explanation of Other Options:
A. Yes – Incorrect. While the Network Contributor role allows managing network-related resources such as NSGs, it does not provide permissions to configure or access Log Analytics, which is essential for enabling Traffic Analytics. Therefore, this role alone is insufficient.
