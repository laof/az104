You need to ensure that an Azure Active Directory (Azure AD) user named Admin1 is assigned the required role to enable Traffic Analytics for an Azure subscription.
Solution: You assign the Reader role at the subscription level to Admin1.
Does this meet the goal?

  • A..Yes
  • CorrectB..No
Suggested Answer:

Question 2-35

Correct Answer: B. No – The Reader role at the subscription level provides read-only access to resources but does not grant sufficient permissions to configure or enable Traffic Analytics. Admin1 would need additional permissions, particularly on the Log Analytics workspace and network resources.
-----------------
Explanation of Other Options:
A. Yes – Incorrect. The Reader role only allows viewing resources and does not allow making changes or enabling features like Traffic Analytics. It lacks the necessary permissions to configure network monitoring or analytics settings.
